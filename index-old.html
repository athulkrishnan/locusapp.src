FAQs

NearU is an Offers and Discounts app backed by high-end Location Intelligence showcasing the best of deals at retail outlets around you

Do I need to Register?
Yes! Registration is like any other account login with your verified phone number & Email ID.

Can I browse without creating a profile?
Absolutely! But to let us know you better & allow you to access the exclusive deals, you will be required to register.

Does my location need to be on?
To suggest deals for you accurately as per your location, we need your GPS to be on. Nevertheless, the city is yours & so are all the deals.

How am I assured of my information being protected?
The Privacy Policy of NearU takes care of that.

Are the offers online or offline or both?
The offers are only offline (in-store) Scoot!

How do I personalize my search?
High on personalization, NearU wants you to ‘ <3’ – Favourite your Preferred Categories, Brands & Outlets! Always get more of what you like!

Do I need to buy a deal?
Nah! No Selling, Only sharing!

If the deals are real time, what about the offer validity & other details?
Right below the offer you will find the offer validity period, terms & conditions along with all other necessary details.

How do I subscribe to an offer?
All you need to do is click on the barcode / QR code for your code to Flash on your screen.

How do I redeem an offer?
Just flash your Bar code or Coupon code at the billing counter.

As a vendor /merchant, is it possible for me to push an offer real time?
NearU puts your business in your hands - You have the power to launch marketing campaigns or simple saver plans in a matter of minutes.
